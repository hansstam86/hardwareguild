U2 · SPEC

What the Guild is

A place where Europe's hardware builders hang out, help each other, and show what they are making. No gatekeeping, no theory-only talk. Bring a project, a question, or a part number. Everything open, everything shared. The Guild runs on people teaching people.

U6 · THE CODE

The Guild code

01

Be decent. Everyone here is learning, including the youngest members. Talk to them the way you would want an adult to talk to your kid.

02

Keep it in the open. Help happens in public channels, so everyone learns from it. No pulling members into private DMs.

03

Give credit. Open source runs on attribution. Name your sources, share your files, pay it forward.

04

No selling, no spam. Share what you built, not what you are flogging. This is a workshop, not a marketplace.

05

Report anything off. Message a moderator or email hans.stam@gmail.com. We read it, and we act on it.
